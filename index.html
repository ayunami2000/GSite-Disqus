Google Sites and Disqus

How to use:

  • Create a Disqus account.
  • When creating the account, choose to integrate Disqus with a site.
  • Fill out both text fields and press the green button.
  • Choose the universal code option for the site type chooser thing.
  • Now, go to Google Sites and place the cursor where you want the Disqus comment area to be.
  • Click on Insert->More Gadgets and click on Add gadget by site.
